The Missing Link — What is an API?

API: Application Programming Interface

The structured digital protocol connecting your client browser with remote computational resources. It serves as a secure courier transmitting your parameters.

To unlock it, you just need a digital passport: The API Key. It authenticates your requests and grants you access to cloud-based intelligence.
